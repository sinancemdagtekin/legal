Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026.

1. Who we are

Reply Coach ("the app", "we", "us") is a mobile application that generates reply suggestions using AI. This policy explains what information we handle when you use the app, why we handle it, and what rights you have. Questions? MY_EMAIL.

2. Information we handle

We handle only what is strictly necessary to run the app:

  • Input text you submit. When you tap "Process", the text you enter is sent to our backend, then relayed to a third-party large-language-model provider (currently OpenAI) that generates the reply suggestions.
  • Device identifier. A stable identifier derived from your device (Android ID on Android) is generated on first launch and used only to count how many free generations you have used, so the free-tier limit is enforced consistently across sessions. It is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • Subscription state. If you subscribe, Google Play and RevenueCat handle the transaction and share the subscription status (active / expired) with our backend so we know whether to skip the free-tier limit.

We do not ask for or collect: your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, camera or microphone data, advertising identifiers, or any social-media data. There are no user accounts and no login.

3. How we use it

  • Input text is used only to generate the reply suggestions you see on screen and is not stored on our servers beyond the seconds needed to process a single request.
  • Device identifier is used only to meter free-tier usage against a per-device counter. It is not shared with any third party, advertising network, or analytics service.
  • Subscription state is used only to determine whether to charge the free-tier limit against your device.

4. Third-party services

The app relies on the following third parties. Their processing of information is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI — receives the text you submit in order to generate reply suggestions. Per OpenAI's API policies at the time of writing, API inputs are not used to train models. See https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.
  • Google Play Billing — handles the purchase, renewal, and cancellation of subscriptions. Google receives the standard purchase data required to process a payment. See https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat — a subscription-management service that relays your subscription status from Google Play to our backend. RevenueCat receives your device identifier (used as an anonymous appUserID) and the subscription events sent by Google. See https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

5. How long we keep it

  • Input text: not retained. It exists only in transit and in memory for the duration of the request.
  • Device identifier + usage counter + subscription state: retained on our backend as long as the app is installed on your device, or until you request deletion (see section 7).

6. Your rights (GDPR — EEA/UK, and Turkey KVKK — Law No. 6698)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Turkey, applicable law (GDPR Articles 15–22 and Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK"), Articles 11–14) gives you the following rights with respect to personal data we hold about you:

  • The right to know whether we process personal data about you and, if so, to receive a copy.
  • The right to have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected.
  • The right to have your data erased ("right to be forgotten").
  • The right to restrict or object to processing in specific circumstances.
  • The right to data portability, where applicable.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority — for EEA users your national Data Protection Authority, and for Turkey the Kişisel Verileri Koruma Kurulu (KVKK, kvkk.gov.tr).

Because we do not collect names, emails, or accounts, in practice the only piece of personal data we hold that could be linked to you is your device identifier plus its associated usage counter and subscription state.

7. Data deletion requests

To request deletion of any data associated with your device, email MY_EMAIL from any address and include the words "Delete my data" along with the device identifier shown in the app's settings screen (or reset by uninstalling the app). We will process your request within 30 days as required by GDPR Article 12(3), and within the timelines set out in KVKK Article 13 for Turkish residents.

8. Children

Reply Coach is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly process data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we will delete the associated device data.

9. International transfers

Our backend is hosted in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany). Requests to OpenAI, Google Play, and RevenueCat may be routed to their servers in the United States or other countries. Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA/UK/Turkey, the third-party providers rely on the transfer mechanisms disclosed in their own privacy policies (typically the EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

10. Security

Communication between the app and our backend uses HTTPS. Communication between our backend and third-party providers is also HTTPS. We do not store input text, so there is no persistent record of what you type that could be breached.

11. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a material way, we will update the effective date at the top and, where required, notify users through the app. The current version is always available at this URL.
